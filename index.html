FAQ

What makes WizeMice different?

Most pedals are fixed systems. WizeMice is open. You can install plugins and expand it over time.

Do I need a computer to use it?

No. It runs standalone. You can connect to a computer or phone if you want more control.

What instruments does it support?

Anything. Guitar, bass, synths, vocals, MIDI.

Can I use my own plugins?

Yes. From our store or anywhere online.

What about analog tone?

You can add analog FX cards for real analog processing.
