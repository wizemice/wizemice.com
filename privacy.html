Privacy Policy

Last updated: 5 May 2026

This policy describes how WizeMice (“we”, “us”) handles personal information when you use our website at wizemice.com (the “Site”) and when you join our product waitlist for WizeCore.

Who is responsible?

The controller of your personal data is WizeMice. Questions about this policy or your data: contact@wizemice.com.

What we collect

Depending on how you use the Site and the waitlist form, we may process:

  • Email address — required to join the waitlist.
  • Name — if you choose to provide it.
  • Role information — if you tell us how you engage with music (optional answers).
  • Technical data — such as browser type and similar information routinely sent when you load a webpage (typically via hosting or infrastructure logs).
  • Connection data when you subscribe — when you submit the waitlist form, your device sends a network request to Mailchimp. Normal internet routing reveals an IP address for that moment. Providers like Mailchimp commonly keep such technical identifiers and may infer a very general geographic area from an IP address (typically not GPS-level location). Mailchimp’s documentation explains its practices more fully than this policy can summarise.

How we use it and legal bases

We use this information to:

  • Operate and improve the Site.
  • Maintain a waitlist and send you updates about WizeCore and related WizeMice news.
  • Respect your preferences and comply with our legal obligations (for example responding to lawful requests).

Where required by applicable law (for example under the GDPR in the EU/UK), we rely on your consent for waitlist signup and associated email updates when you subscribe. You may withdraw consent at any time using the unsubscribe link in our emails or by contacting us at the email above.

Email marketing provider

We use Intuit Mailchimp to collect waitlist entries and deliver messages. Mailchimp processes your data as a service provider acting on our instructions. Mailchimp’s own privacy notice is published at: intuit.com/privacy/statement.

Retention

We keep waitlist-related data while you remain subscribed or until we no longer need it for our legitimate purposes, consistent with Mailchimp’s capabilities and applicable law.

Transfers

Mailchimp and infrastructure providers may store or process information in countries other than yours, including the United States. Where required, appropriate safeguards apply (such as standard contractual clauses published by regulators).

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data, and to restrict or object to certain processing. You may also lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. Contact us using the email above and we’ll respond consistent with applicable law.

Cookies and similar technologies

This Site loads fonts from Google Fonts; Google may receive technical connection data typical of web requests. We do not run optional advertising trackers on this Site.

Children

The Site is not directed at individuals under the age specified by applicable privacy law.

Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will appear on this page with a revised date. For material changes we may notify you where appropriate.
